# Exploratory Interpretation: Small-Scale-Factor Refinement

## Scope

This interpretation note applies to
`config_small_scale_factor.yaml` only. It does not modify the scientific status
of the official two-bin `minisuperspace_frw` baseline.

## What the Outputs Mean

When benchmarked exactly, this refinement reports expectation values for the
retained scale factor, the retained volume proxy, the projected Hamiltonian, and
the occupation probability of the smallest retained positive-scale-factor bin.

These are observables of a finite four-bin truncation only.

## What the Outputs Do Not Establish

The outputs do not establish:

- a literal Planck-Epoch simulation,
- a complete quantum-cosmology wave function,
- a full Wheeler-DeWitt solution,
- realistic singularity resolution in nature,
- full Planck-scale quantum gravity.

The Planck-Epoch label remains contextual only. Any scientific claim must stay
tied to the explicit reduced operator, truncation, encoding, and benchmark.
